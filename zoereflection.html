In my opinion something negative that I learnt from this assignment and researching the idea of microchipping humans was the idea of identity theft. It’s as easy as a click of a button almost, which can be a terrifying idea and aspect of being microchipped. This is a major negative as these microchips hold a major amount of personal data so that the chip is personalised for the user as well as it being used for identification just like a driver’s license.
